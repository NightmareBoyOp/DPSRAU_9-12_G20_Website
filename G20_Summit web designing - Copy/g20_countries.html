G20 Countries and Leaders

India

India is a land of various cultures and a rich heritage. It is the seventh-largest country by area and the second-most populous country globally. The peacock is India’s national bird, and the Bengal tiger is the country’s national animal. The national song is named Vande Matram (written by Bankimchandra Chatterji). The Indian national song was first performed at the Indian National Congress in 1896. ‘Jana Gana Mana,’ India’s national anthem, is sung in 52 seconds. The national flag of India is named Tiranga, which is made up of three colours: saffron, white, and green, with the Ashoka Chakra in navy blue in the centre. India's leader is Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who has been in office since 2014.

United States

The United States of America, commonly known as the United States or America, is a transcontinental country located primarily in North America. It consists of 50 states, a federal district, five major unincorporated territories, nine minor outlying islands, and 326 Indian reservations. The country is led by President Joe Biden, who took office in 2021.

China

China, the most populous country in the world, is located in East Asia. It has a rapidly growing economy and is led by President Xi Jinping.

Germany

Germany is a European powerhouse known for its strong economy and technological innovation. The country is led by Chancellor Angela Merkel.

Japan

Japan, an island nation in East Asia, is known for its advanced technology and culture. The country is led by Prime Minister Yoshihide Suga.

Brazil

Brazil, located in South America, is known for its diverse ecosystems and the Amazon Rainforest. The country is led by President Jair Bolsonaro.

Russia

Russia, the largest country in the world, spans Eastern Europe and northern Asia. The country is led by President Vladimir Putin.

France

France, in Western Europe, is known for its rich history, culture, and cuisine. The country is led by President Emmanuel Macron.

Italy

Italy, located in Southern Europe, is famous for its art, history, and cuisine. The country is led by Prime Minister Mario Draghi.

United Kingdom

The United Kingdom, comprising England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland, has a rich history and cultural heritage. The country is led by Prime Minister Boris Johnson.

Australia

Australia, a continent-country, is known for its unique wildlife and landscapes. The country is led by Prime Minister Scott Morrison.

South Africa

South Africa, located in southern Africa, is known for its diverse cultures and natural beauty. The country is led by President Cyril Ramaphosa.

Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia is a Middle Eastern country known for its vast deserts and oil reserves. The country is led by King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud.

Canada

Canada, located in North America, is known for its stunning landscapes and multicultural society. The country is led by Prime Minister Justin Trudeau.

South Korea

South Korea, in East Asia, is known for its technological advancements and vibrant culture. The country is led by President Moon Jae-in.

Turkey

Turkey, a transcontinental country, is known for its unique blend of Eastern and Western cultures. The country is led by President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan.

Argentina

Argentina, located in South America, is known for its passion for soccer and rich history. The country is led by President Alberto Fernández.

Indonesia

Indonesia, an archipelago in Southeast Asia, is known for its diverse culture and natural beauty. The country is led by President Joko Widodo.

Mexico

Mexico, in North America, is known for its vibrant traditions and delicious cuisine. The country is led by President Andrés Manuel López Obrador.
